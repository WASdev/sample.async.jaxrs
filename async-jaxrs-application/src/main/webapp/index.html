Here are some sample services that can illustrate how JAX-RS works with and without async behaviors. In all cases, the client (this browser window) is making synchronous requests to the server. Click a link, count to two, and the response will come back. We could make it fancier, but this sample isn't really about the client side behavior.

The interesting activity is in the server, and is best observed by watching the output of the server (the console window of WDT, or standard out or console.log).
